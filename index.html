ABOUT

I am Systems Engineering graduate from Simon Fraser University (SFU) in Burnaby, British Columbia Canada.

I am originally from Kenya and as a kid I was always curious of how electronics such as TVs worked. This curiosity gradually developed into a strong interest and passion for technology and how it can positively impact my community.

My latest experience was with Thales Canada as a System Integration Test Engineer, where I worked on their Communication Based Train Control system (CBTC).

My goal is to become a Systems Engineer mainly focussing on Requirement Engineering and System Design, in the the next 3 to 4 years. I would like have a strong understanding of the Business Needs of the company I am a part of, and contribute in building sustainable projects through Systems Engineering Life Cycle Model.
